Location

After logging in, on the home page clicking on the circle with the avatar (with the student's name next to it) in the upper left corner, we are taken to the Profile page:

Feature description

In the Profile page we have:

Honey Pot

Every time the student completes a topic, they earn honey pots and stars.

The Honey Pot points are used to unlock new avatars.

Honey Pot points are consumables.

Stars

Every time the student completes a topic, they earn honey pots and stars.

Stars received vary depending on how well the student has responded to the topic questions.

Shop

In the Shop we can see the avatars already unlocked and buy new ones.

The student can change their avatar here, by clicking on the check of available avatars:

Tutorial

The Tutorial can also be accessed from the Profile page, by clicking int he button located in the lower left corner:
